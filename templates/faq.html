2. Leave Management

Can I increase my Team’s monthly Annual Leave Days increment?

Yes, the basic according to Basic Labor Conditions is 1.25 Days a month. You have an option to increase it to what meets your business requirements or nature of your Business.

Can I cancel an Employee’s Leave that has been approved already?

Yes, however you must manually increase all the Leave Days you had deducted.

3. Payroll & Tax

What Payroll Reports does the Connect HR system generate?

Connect HR generates ACB, EMP201, EMP501 and IRP5 reports.

Can the system generate different pay schedules according to my business need?

Yes, our systems support weekly, fortnightly, and monthly payroll schedules. When you set up your Company Profile you will choose which one will be most applicable to your business.

4. Expense Manager

Who benefits from an Expense Manager feature?

Both employers and employees benefit. Employers save time on manual processing and gain better visibility into spending, while employees experience quicker reimbursements and less hassle with submissions.

Can employees submit expense claims directly through the system?

Yes, employees can upload receipts and submit claims through the software, often using a mobile app or web interface.
