About Us

Connect HR is a 100% black-owned South African company that provides an integrated, web-based Human Resource management system designed to streamline HR processes for small businesses, companies employing minimum-wage workers, frontline staff, and HR consultants servicing small businesses. Our system covers all aspects of HR management, including payroll, leave management, document control, tax calculations, compliance reporting, expense management, and more.
