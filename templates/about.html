About Us

Connect HR is a 100% black-owned South African company that provides an integrated, web-based Human Resource management system designed to streamline HR processes for small businesses, companies employing minimum-wage workers, frontline staff, and HR consultants servicing small businesses.

Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative, efficient, and compliant HR solutions that empower businesses of all sizes — from small businesses and frontline employers to HR consultants — with the tools they need to manage their workforce seamlessly.

Mission

Our mission is to simplify and automate HR tasks, allowing businesses to focus on growth while ensuring compliance with South Africa’s labour laws and tax regulations. We offer a comprehensive, affordable, and user-friendly solution that scales with your business.
